ABOUT

Joshua Photo Studio is an independent photography studio based in Italy, led by Joshua.

With a quiet, emotional, and timeless approach, Joshua documents weddings, portraits, and intimate stories through natural light, soft tones, and a cinematic eye.

The work is created for people who value honest moments, elegant details, and photographs that remain beautiful not only today, but for years to come.
