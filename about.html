ABOUT

Where light
turns into
memory.

An independent photography studio shaped by emotion, stillness, and the quiet beauty of Florence.

I was born in China. In 2024, I chose to move to Italy and began living in Florence, a city surrounded by Renaissance art, history, and light.

Photography, for me, is not only about recording what appears in front of the camera. It is about preserving the feeling of a person, a relationship, or a moment that once quietly existed.

Today, Joshua Photo Studio works as an independent photography studio, focusing on weddings, portraits, couples, and intimate life stories. My images are guided by natural light, quiet emotion, and a sense of time.
